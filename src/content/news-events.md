## 2025

### MVPI Annual Appreciation Gala 2025

**Date:** February 19, 2025
**Location:** M Resort & Hotel, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

MVP International Capital successfully hosted the MVPI Annual Appreciation Banquet & IIFLE 1st Anniversary, bringing together nearly 100 distinguished guests including representatives from Malaysia-listed companies, Nasdaq-listed companies, U.S. listing professionals, local industry experts, pre-IPO companies, and VIPs.

**Event Highlights:**
- Nasdaq IPO Advisory Initiation Engagement Signing Ceremony
- **Smart Rental** (IT subscription-based company) signed by CEO Mr. Joshua Chin
- **Pine Hills International School** signed by Vice President Mr. Jonathan Goh Shyh Yong
- MVP International Capital represented by Mr. Freeman Lau, Chairman & Managing Director

**2025 Goals Announced:**
- MVPI ASEAN targets signing and engaging **30 companies** for Nasdaq IPOs in 2025

**Source:** [FinancialContent](https://markets.financialcontent.com/stocks/article/marketersmedia-2025-3-3-mvpi-annual-appreciation-gala-2025-pioneering-nasdaq-success-and-regional-expansion-ahead)

### Monarch Equity Capital Partnership

**Date:** May 2024

Monarch Equity Capital Berhad entered into a collaboration agreement with MVP International Capital (MVPI). Under this partnership, MVPI will provide expert financial advisory services with a specific focus on facilitating Monarch Equity's Nasdaq listing process.

**Target:** US$100 Million for Nasdaq Listing

**Source:** [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/monarch-equity-capital-berhad-targets-023200799.html)

### Kohai (IWG Esports) IPO Advisory Initiation

**Date:** 2024

IWG Esports, the esports organisation behind the Kohai Brand in Malaysia, Singapore, and Indonesia, initiated its Nasdaq IPO advisory with MVPI Capital. The company has registered with the aim of raising $2 million in its Series A.
