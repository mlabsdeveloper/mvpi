### Company Story
Wang & Lee's story began in 1981 when Wang & Lee Engineering Limited was founded, initially conducting electrical work. As Hong Kong's construction industry boomed, the company expanded its scope significantly. By 1990, they had provided services for numerous shops, factories, and residential buildings.

On December 3, 1992, Wang & Lee Contracting Limited (formerly Wang & Lee Engineering M/E Limited) was incorporated.
